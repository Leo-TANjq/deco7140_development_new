Style Guide

A style guide outlining my design across three distinct levels is presented below.

The style guide will help me design and develop a consistent and cohesive user experience across my website, a subculture and hobby community. It can be divided into three different levels:

Visceral

Designing for visually appealing and engaging content. It focuses on typography, color schemes, layout and so on, following the principles of esthetics and web design.

Learn More
Behavioural

Designing for usability and functionality. It focuses on how users interact with the content, like buttons, providing a seamless and intuitive experience for users.

Learn More
Reflective

Focusing on the deeper meaning and emotional connection users have with the content. It aims to create a lasting impression and foster a sense of loyalty and trust with the users.
