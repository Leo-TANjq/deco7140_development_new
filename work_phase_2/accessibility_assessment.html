Accessibility Assessment

Success Criteria Analysis

1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)

Normally the visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1.

Benefits

People with low vision often have difficulty reading text that does not contrast with its background. Providing a minimum luminance contrast ratio between the text and its background can make the text more readable even if the person does not see the full range of colors. It also works for the rare individuals who see no color.

Relevant Needs

Requirement

Make sure text stands out from the background, especially for people with vision issues.

Source

Ethan - Network Engineer from user stories.

Implementation

Provide sufficient contrast between text and its background by using consistent color combinations. For example, use dark text on a light background or light text on a dark background.

2.1.1 Keyboard Navigation

All functionality of the content is operable through a keyboard interface without requiring specific timings for individual keystrokes, except where the underlying function requires input that depends on the path of the user's movement and not just the endpoints.

Benefits
  • People who are blind (who cannot use devices such as mice that require eye-hand coordination)
  • People with low vision (who may have trouble finding or tracking a pointer indicator on screen )
  • Some people with hand tremors find using a mouse very difficult and therefore usually use a keyboard
Relevant Needs

Requirement 1

The site should be fully navigable using the keyboard alone. This is essential for those who cannot use a mouse or touch screen.

3.2.3 Consistent Navigation

Navigational mechanisms that are repeated on multiple Web pages within a set of Web pages occur in the same relative order each time they are repeated, unless a change is initiated by the user.

Benefits

The benefit of consistent navigation is that it helps users to learn the interface and find information more quickly . It also helps to reduce the cognitive load on users, as they do not have to relearn the navigation each time they visit a new page.
